Fluffy French Toast

This French toast recipe is different because it uses flour. I have given it to some friends and they've all liked it better than the French toast they usually make!

Nutrition info

  • Prep: 10 mins
  • Cook: 20 mins
  • Total: 30 mins
  • Servings: 12
  • Yield: 12 slices

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon gound cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon white suger
  • 12 thick slices bread

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Measure flour into a large mixing bowl. slowly whisk in the milk. whisk in the salt, eggs, cinnamon, vanilla extract, and suger until smooth.

  2. Step 2

    3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat.

  3. Step 3

    4. Soak bread slices in the mixture until saturated. Cook bread on each side until golden brown. Serve hot.


Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 123 calories; protein 4.8g; Carbohydrates 19.4; fat 2.7g; cholesterol 48.1mg; sodium 230.2mg.
